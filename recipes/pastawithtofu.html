Pasta with Tofu and Zucchini (Lazy)

Description

A simple pasta dish for all occasions :)

Ingredients for one

  • 100gr of whatever type of pasta you fancy
  • 100gr of tofu
  • 2 spoons of soy sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 spoon smoked paprika
  • 1 spoon olive oil
  • 1 pot of water

Steps

  1. Place the pot with water on the stove and start bringing it to a boil
  2. While you wait squeeze the block of tofu with your hands in a bowl until it's roughly crumbled
  3. Soak it with the soy sauce and smoked paprika, let it sit
  4. Chop the garlic and zucchini to your liking
  5. When the water boils remember to salt it and put the pasta in! Then let it boil for the amount of time indicated on the package
  6. In a pan heat up the olive oil and let the garlic fry there a lil bit
  7. Add the zucchini and let them cook
  8. When the zucchini are cooked but not yet soft add the marinated tofu
  9. If it gets too dry you can add some pasta water
  10. When the pasta is cooked don't throw the pasta water just yet you might need some!
  11. Add the pasta to the pan and mix it with the other ingredients, add some cooking water to your desired moistness
  12. All done!!! Serve it up!!
