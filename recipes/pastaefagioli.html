Pasta e Fagioli (Lazy)

Description

A warm italian soup made with simple and cheap ingredients :)

Ingredients for one

  • 250gr of canned beans
  • 200gr of tomato puree
  • 60 gr of pasta (small size)
  • 1 cup of water (or a little more, as needed)
  • 1 vegetable stock cube (or less, as needed)
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 spoon olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop the onions
  2. Heat the oil in a pan and throw the onions in
  3. Wait until onions are transparent then throw the puree in
  4. Add the beans and the vegetable cube
  5. Add the water bring it to a boil!
  6. Taste it a lil bit to see if the stock is flavourful enough (don't burn your tongue pls)
  7. Add the pasta to boiling broth
  8. Check the time the pasta takes to cook and wait that amount stirring it sometimes
  9. All done a lazy meal is ready for you!!
