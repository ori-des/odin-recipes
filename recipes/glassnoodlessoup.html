Glass Noodles Soup (Lazy)

Description

A quick warm bowl of soup with veggies :)

Ingredients for one

  • 100gr silken tofu
  • 80gr glass noodles
  • 6 mushrooms
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 spoon miso paste

Steps

  1. Heat up the water to a boil in a pot, add vegetable stock cube when boiling
  2. Chop the mushrooms, the bell pepper and the carrot
  3. Add to boiling water and let them cook until slightly soft
  4. Cut the silken tofu into cubes, add to water when vegetables are cooked
  5. Add noodles to soup and let them cook following the instructions on the package
  6. With the heat turned off add the miso paste and let it dissolve in the broth
  7. All done!! Serve it up!!!
